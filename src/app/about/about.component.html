About Transwat Techno Engineering Services Limited (TTESL)

Transwat Techno Engineering Services Limited (TTESL), est. 1960, with its Registered Office in Mumbai, has completed consultancy for over 5000 projects across India in the last 65 years, covering varied sectors including: Transportation, Water, Buildings, Industrial, Logistics, and more. Transportation
