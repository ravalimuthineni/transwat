Highways, Bridges & Expressways

Development of road networks in India is essential not only for supporting the economy but it also facilitates easy and economical mode of transport for all classes of the society, faster and comprehensive reach to the markets.Transwat Techno Engineering formerly Wadia Techno-Engineering Services Ltd has executed many projects in transportation sector.

With 60+ years of expertise, Transwat has delivered world-class infrastructure across India:

Our Expertise

  • Highways, Expressways & Elevated Corridors: Comprehensive design and supervision of highways and expressways using both flexible and rigid pavement technologies.
  • Flyovers, ROBs & Major Bridges: Specialized consultancy in designing flyovers, Railway Over Bridges (ROBs), major river bridges, and grade separators.
  • Urban Roads & Smart Mobility Corridors: Development of city roads, urban logistics corridors, and smart arterial routes designed for efficient urban mobility.
  • Feasibility Studies, DPRs & Project Management: End-to-end support including feasibility analysis, preparation of Detailed Project Reports (DPRs), bid process management, and Authority Engineer services.

Dams, Reservoirs & Lift Irrigation

Transwat has been pivotal in water infrastructure:

  • Dams, Canals, Reservoirs, Tunnels, and Lift Irrigation Systems
  • Handled over 69 EPC Major Irrigation Projects valued above ₹10,000 Cr
  • Specialized QC services, feasibility, and DPRs

Highlights

Expertise includes radial gates, embankment dams, canal networks, and irrigation tunnels. Transwat's involvement in the **JALAYAGNAM** program in Andhra Pradesh is a key highlight, where they provided design review and multi-level Quality Control for numerous major irrigation projects, including over 10 Reservoirs, 4 Dams, 3 Tunnels (two of which were 18 km long and utilized Tunnel Boring Machines), Lift Irrigation Schemes, and Main canals.

Specific projects include:

  • Dams: Sunkesula NOF Dam (378.50m long, 59.652m max height), Kakarla NOF Dam (356m long, 60m max height), Gottipadia NOF Dam (587m long, 86.14m max height), and Gandikota Dam (19,197 Sq. Km catchment area, 26.85 TMC gross storage, with 14 gates).
  • Tunnels: Design review for Tunnel-I (18,820m long, 7.00m diameter) and Tunnel-II (18,838m long, 9.20m diameter), both with significant progress in boring and segment casting, and focused defect rectification.
  • Micro & Lift Irrigation Systems: Expertise in optimizing piped irrigation networks, pump houses, and intake structures, adhering to CPHEEO Manual and CWC guidelines for efficient design.
